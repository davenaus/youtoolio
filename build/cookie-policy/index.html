Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our service. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and other browser storage mechanisms.

How We Use Cookies

YouTool.io uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Functionality: To provide core features and maintain your session
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our tools
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and search history
  • Performance: To optimize loading times and user experience

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and security.

Purpose: Session management, security, basic functionality
Duration: Session or until browser is closed
Can be disabled: No - required for site operation

Analytics Cookies

Optional

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about usage patterns.

Purpose: Google Analytics, Vercel Analytics, usage tracking
Duration: Up to 2 years
Can be disabled: Yes - through browser settings or ad blockers

Preference Cookies

Optional

These cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide a personalized experience across visits.

Purpose: Search history, tool preferences, UI settings
Duration: Persistent until manually cleared
Can be disabled: Yes - through browser settings

Local Storage Usage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage for:

  • Search History: Your recent searches for convenience
  • Tool Preferences: Settings and filters you've applied
  • Session Data: Temporary data during your visit
  • Cache: Temporary storage to improve performance

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and usage tracking
  • Vercel Analytics: For performance monitoring
  • YouTube API: For accessing video and channel data

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block All Cookies: Prevent all cookies from being stored
  • Delete Cookies: Remove existing cookies from your device
  • Third-Party Cookies: Block cookies from external domains
  • Session Cookies: Only allow temporary cookies

Clear YouTool.io Data

Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or clearing your data.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Site may not function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: We can't improve the service based on usage data
  • Preference Cookies: Settings won't be remembered between visits

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our practices. We will post any updates on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
