How to Use the Channel Consultant

Our Channel Consultant creates a custom AI assistant trained specifically on your YouTube channel. The AI understands your niche, audience, and branding to provide personalized content strategy, SEO optimization, and creative assistance tailored to your unique channel goals.

1

Enter Channel URL

Paste your YouTube channel URL in the search field above. Our system analyzes your channel details, category, description, and subscriber metrics to understand your content focus and audience demographics.

2

AI Bot Creation

The system generates comprehensive instructions that train an AI chatbot to become your personal YouTube consultant. The bot understands your niche and can operate in four specialized modes: SEO, Ideation, Script, and Trendjack.

3

Deploy Your Consultant

Copy the generated instructions and paste them into any AI platform like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. Your custom consultant will immediately understand your channel and provide expert advice tailored to your specific content strategy needs.

AI Consultant Modes

  • SEO Mode: Generates optimized titles, descriptions, tags, and thumbnail concepts based on video content
  • Ideation Mode: Creates video ideas and content concepts specifically aligned with your channel niche
  • Script Mode: Writes detailed 5,000-word scripts for any topic within your content area
  • Trendjack Mode: Identifies upcoming events, launches, and trends relevant to your niche for timely content
  • Personalized Insights: All recommendations consider your channel's specific category, audience, and content style
  • Multi-Platform Support: Works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other AI platforms

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which AI platform should I use the generated instructions with?   • What is the difference between the four modes? SEO Mode generates titles, descriptions, tags, and thumbnail concepts from a video transcript or topic. Trendjack Mode searches for upcoming events, product launches, and trends relevant to your content area that you can create timely videos about.
  • How often should I regenerate my consultant? Regenerate your consultant whenever your channel undergoes a significant change ‚Äî a niche pivot, a major subscriber milestone, a shift in upload frequency, or a change in your top-performing content type.   • Does the tool work for brand new channels with no videos? The consultant works best when your channel has at least a few videos, since it analyzes your recent uploads and top performers to personalize recommendations. For brand new channels, you can still generate instructions ‚Äî the bot will use your channel description and category to inform its advice, though it will have less historical data to draw from.
